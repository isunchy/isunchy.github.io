Biography

I am a fifth-year Ph.D candidate of Institute for Advanced Study, Tsinghua University. Currently, I'm in a joint Ph.D program of Tsinghua University and Microsoft Research Asia. My supervisors are Dr. Yang Liu and Dr. Xin Tong. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor's degree from Xidian University in 2015.

My research interest includes data-driven shape analysis and computer graphics.
